Motivation

After creating my life as a Pixar story exercise, I was blown away by what Sora could do. I wanted to make it easy for anyone to turn their daily calendar events into videos—like having a creator and videographer by your side to capture your daily highlights.

Solution

I built a simple, elegant product. Users connect their Google Calendar, choose which days they want to turn into a movie, and voila—they can share a day of their life with friends and loved ones.

The technical build was straightforward: Google Calendar feeds in the events, GPT writes a script, and Sora generates the video. For the UI, I wanted a warm, creative feeling, so I took inspiration from Airbnb. After 2-3 hours of vibe coding with Claude Code and hooking up the APIs, I had it running.

The only bottleneck Claude Code and I hit was setting up Firebase Storage to store and display the videos. Originally, Claude Code assumed the Sora API would return a URL we could display directly—turns out we needed the extra storage layer.
