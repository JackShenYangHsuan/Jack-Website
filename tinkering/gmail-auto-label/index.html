Gmail auto-label assistant

Problem

  • Email assistants like Fyxer and Perplexity are helpful but overkill for low email volume
  • Only need the auto-labelling feature, not the full suite
  • Don't want to pay $20/month for a sixth AI subscription

Solution

  • Built a Gmail auto-labelling system with customizable rules
  • Works for both received and sent emails
  • Highly configurable labelling logic without the subscription cost

Challenge

  • Initially ran all emails through the same rule set
  • GPT couldn't determine who sent the last email in long threads
  • Often confused "to respond" vs "awaiting reply" states
  • Fixed by adding a function layer that first identifies sent vs received
  • Then applies appropriate rules, making GPT more accurate
