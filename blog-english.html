Now is the time to master English

October 2024

AI collapsed the barrier between human thought and machine execution. The new competitive advantage is language precision.

Productivity equals human output plus machine output over time. Human capacity caps quickly. Massive productivity gains come from amplifying what machines can do.

For decades, we instructed machines through code or software interfaces—both requiring technical expertise and limiting communication bandwidth. AI removed these constraints. We now instruct machines with natural language, just like we communicate with people.

This changes everything. Those who articulate thoughts structurally and precisely connect their biological brains with unlimited machine capacity. They have machines execute and expand their ideas at scale.

Your imagination no longer faces practical limits. You can create software, images, videos in seconds by expressing creativity clearly through language.

Now is the time to master English communication. It makes you a better leader and mobilizer. More critically, it makes you the commander of an infinite army of intelligent machines.
